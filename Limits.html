Different Kinds of Discontinuity


There are three kinds of discontinuity, removable, jump, and infinite
A jump discontinuity takes place in a piecewise function where the limit from the left and right are not equal
A removable discontinuity takes place in a piecewise function where the limit from the left and right are equal but the graph is undefined or redefined at that point
an infinite discontinuity occurs at an asymptote
